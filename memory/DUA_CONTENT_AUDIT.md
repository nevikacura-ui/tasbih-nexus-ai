### 1. In the Name  _(id: `r5_01_bismillah`)_

- **Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ
- **Transliteration**: Bismillāhir-Rahmānir-Raheem
- **English**: In the name of Allah, the most Beneficent, the most Merciful.

### 2. O Believers  _(id: `r5_02_ya_ayyuhal`)_

- **Arabic**: يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا
- **Transliteration**: Yā ayyuhal-lazeena āmanū
- **English**: O you who believe!

### 3. Do Not Betray  _(id: `r5_03_takhoonu`)_

- **Arabic**: لَا تَخُونُوا اللَّهَ وَالرَّسُولَ
- **Transliteration**: Lā takhoonullāha war-Rasūla
- **English**: Do not betray Allah and the Apostle.

### 4. Your Trusts  _(id: `r5_04_amanat`)_

- **Arabic**: وَتَخُونُوا أَمَانَاتِكُمْ
- **Transliteration**: Wa takhoonū amānātikum
- **English**: And (do not) betray your trusts.

### 5. While You Know  _(id: `r5_05_talamoon`)_

- **Arabic**: وَأَنْتُمْ تَعْلَمُونَ
- **Transliteration**: Wa antum ta'lamoon
- **English**: While you know.

### 6. Forgive Us  _(id: `r5_06_rabbanaghfir`)_

- **Arabic**: رَبَّنَا اغْفِرْ لَنَا ذُنُوبَنَا
- **Transliteration**: Rabbanaghfir lanā zunoobanā
- **English**: O our Lord, forgive us our sins.

### 7. Ease Our Tasks  _(id: `r5_07_sahhil`)_

- **Arabic**: وَسَهِّلْ أُمُورَنَا
- **Transliteration**: Wa sah-hil umooranā
- **English**: And make our tasks easy.

### 8. Sustenance & Mercy  _(id: `r5_08_warzuqna`)_

- **Arabic**: وَارْزُقْنَا وَارْحَمْنَا
- **Transliteration**: Warzuqnā warhamnā
- **English**: Give us our daily bread, and have mercy upon us.

### 9. The Omnipotent  _(id: `r5_09_qadeer`)_

- **Arabic**: إِنَّكَ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ
- **Transliteration**: Innaka 'alā kulli shay'in qadeer
- **English**: Verily, You are the Omnipotent.
- 🔸 _Mid-insert (rendered between this and next verse):_ **A whispered call** — `Yā 'Aly yā Muhammad — yā Muhammad yā 'Aly` — O Aly, O Muhammad — O Muhammad, O Aly.

### 10. O Imam of the Time  _(id: `r5_10_imam_zaman`)_

- **Arabic**: يَا إِمَامَ الزَّمَانِ يَا مَوْلَانَا
- **Transliteration**: Yā Imāmaz-Zamān, yā Mawlānā
- **English**: O Imam of the time, O our Lord.

### 11. You Are My Strength  _(id: `r5_11_anta_quwwati`)_

- **Arabic**: أَنْتَ قُوَّتِي
- **Transliteration**: Anta quwwatī
- **English**: You are my strength.

### 12. You Are My Support  _(id: `r5_12_anta_sanadi`)_

- **Arabic**: وَأَنْتَ سَنَدِي
- **Transliteration**: Wa anta sanadī
- **English**: And You are my support.

### 13. On You I Rely  _(id: `r5_13_tikali`)_

- **Arabic**: وَعَلَيْكَ اتِّكَالِي
- **Transliteration**: Wa 'alayka ittikālī
- **English**: And on You I rely.

### 14. O Present, Living  _(id: `r5_14_ya_hazir`)_

- **Arabic**: يَا حَاضِرُ يَا مَوْجُودُ
- **Transliteration**: Yā Hāzir yā Mawjood
- **English**: O present, O living.

### 15. O Shah Rahim  _(id: `r5_15_ya_shah_rahim`)_

- **Arabic**: يَا شَاهْ رَحِيمُ الْحُسَيْنِي
- **Transliteration**: Yā Shāh Rahimul Husaynī
- **English**: O Shah Rahim al-Husayni.

### 16. The True Manifest Imam  _(id: `r5_16_antal_imam`)_

- **Arabic**: أَنْتَ الْإِمَامُ الْحَقُّ الْمُبِينُ
- **Transliteration**: Antal Imāmul Haqqul Mubeen
- **English**: You are the true, manifest Imam.

## Rakaat VI (18 verses)


### 1. In the Name  _(id: `r6_01_bismillah`)_

- **Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ
- **Transliteration**: Bismillāhir-Rahmānir-Raheem
- **English**: In the name of Allah, the most Beneficent, the most Merciful.

### 2. Allah, the One  _(id: `r6_02_qul_huwallah`)_

- **Arabic**: قُلْ هُوَ اللَّهُ أَحَدٌ
- **Transliteration**: Qul Huwallāhu Ahad
- **English**: Say: it is Allah, (Who is) the One.

### 3. The Absolute  _(id: `r6_03_samad`)_

- **Arabic**: اللَّهُ الصَّمَدُ
- **Transliteration**: Allāhus-Samad
- **English**: Allah is Absolute, Independent.

### 4. He Did Not Beget  _(id: `r6_04_lam_yalid`)_

- **Arabic**: لَمْ يَلِدْ
- **Transliteration**: Lam yalid
- **English**: He did not beget.

### 5. Nor Was He Begotten  _(id: `r6_05_lam_yulad`)_

- **Arabic**: وَلَمْ يُولَدْ
- **Transliteration**: Wa lam yūlad
- **English**: Nor was He begotten.

### 6. None Like Him  _(id: `r6_06_kufuwan`)_

- **Arabic**: وَلَمْ يَكُنْ لَهُ كُفُوًا أَحَدٌ
- **Transliteration**: Wa lam yakullahū kufuwan ahad
- **English**: And there is none like unto Him.

### 7. In the Name of Muhammad  _(id: `r6_07_bi_haqqi_muhammad`)_

- **Arabic**: اللَّهُمَّ بِحَقِّ مُحَمَّدٍ الْمُصْطَفَىٰ
- **Transliteration**: Allāhumma bi haqqi Muhammadinil-Mustafā
- **English**: O Allah, in the name of Muhammad — the Chosen.

### 8. And Aly the Favourite  _(id: `r6_08_aly_murtaza`)_

- **Arabic**: وَعَلِيٍّ الْمُرْتَضَىٰ
- **Transliteration**: Wa 'Aliyyinil-Murtazā
- **English**: And Aly — the Favourite.

### 9. Fatima the Radiant  _(id: `r6_09_fatima`)_

- **Arabic**: وَفَاطِمَةَ الزَّهْرَاءِ
- **Transliteration**: Wa Fātimataz-Zahrā
- **English**: And Fatima — the Radiant.

### 10. Hasan & Husayn  _(id: `r6_10_hasan_husayn`)_

- **Arabic**: وَالْحَسَنِ وَالْحُسَيْنِ
- **Transliteration**: Wal Hasani wal Husayn
- **English**: And (Hazrat) Hasan, and (Imam) Husayn.

### 11. Mawlana Aly  _(id: `r6_11_mawlana_aly`)_

- **Arabic**: اللَّهُمَّ بِحَقِّ مَوْلَانَا عَلِيٍّ
- **Transliteration**: Allāhumma bi haqqi Mawlānā 'Aly
- **English**: O Allah, in the name of our Lord Aly.
- 🕌 _Interlude card after this verse_: **Tasbih of the Imams** (50 names)

### 12. Our Lord & Imam  _(id: `r6_12_mawlana_imam`)_

- **Arabic**: وَبِحَقِّ مَوْلَانَا وَإِمَامِنَا الْحَاضِرِ الْمَوْجُودِ
- **Transliteration**: Wa bi haqqi Mawlānā wa Imāminal Hāziril Mawjood
- **English**: And in the name of our Lord and our present, living Imam.

### 13. Have Mercy  _(id: `r6_13_irhamna`)_

- **Arabic**: شَاهْ رَحِيمِ الْحُسَيْنِي ارْحَمْنَا
- **Transliteration**: Shāh Rahimil Husaynī irhamnā
- **English**: Shah Rahim al-Husayni, have mercy upon us.

### 14. Forgive Us  _(id: `r6_14_waghfir`)_

- **Arabic**: وَاغْفِرْ لَنَا
- **Transliteration**: Waghfir lanā
- **English**: And forgive us (our sins).
- 🔸 _Mid-insert (rendered between this and next verse):_ **My Prostration** — `Allāhumma laka sujoodī wa tā-'atī` — O Allah, to You is my prostration and obedience.

### 15. The Omnipotent  _(id: `r6_15_qadeer`)_

- **Arabic**: إِنَّكَ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ
- **Transliteration**: Innaka 'alā kulli shay'in qadeer
- **English**: Verily, You are the Omnipotent.

### 16. All Praise  _(id: `r6_16_walhamdu`)_

- **Arabic**: وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ
- **Transliteration**: Wal-hamdu lillāhi Rabbil 'Ālameen
- **English**: And all praise is due to Allah, the Lord of the worlds.

### 17. Shāh jo Dīdār  _(id: `r6_17_shah_jo_didar`)_

- **Arabic**: شَاهْ جو دِيدَار
- **Transliteration**: Shāh-jo-Dīdār
- **English**: May you be blessed with the holy Dīdār of our Lord.

### 18. My Prostration  _(id: `r6_18_sujoodi`)_

- **Arabic**: اللَّهُمَّ لَكَ سُجُودِي وَطَاعَتِي
- **Transliteration**: Allāhumma laka sujoodī wa tā-'atī
- **English**: O Allah, to You is my prostration and obedience.
